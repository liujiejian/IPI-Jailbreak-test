作者：Shuyan Zhou, Frank F. Xu, Hao Zhu, Xuhui Zhou, Robert Lo, Abishek Sridhar, Xianyi Cheng, Yonatan Bisk, Daniel Fried, Uri Alon, Graham Neubig

Abstract. WebArena provides reproducible, self-hosted websites and tasks for evaluating autonomous web agents on long-horizon, realistic browsing goals.
